COMPANY DESCRIPTION

MLB Trade has been operating since 2015, founded by Dato' Ahmad Ridzuan Bin Hamzah, making 2025 our 10th anniversary in the trading world. Over the past decade, we have achieved excellent performance by generating revenue of more than RM5 million, proof of the success of our technology-based trading strategies.

We are a company that integrates Information Technology (IT) to optimize our trading operations. By leveraging high-tech equipment, sophisticated platforms, and data-driven strategies, we continue to lead the dynamic financial markets.

We have wholeheartedly embraced the digital trading era. Through the use of algorithmic trading, we employ sophisticated algorithms driven by high computing power to analyze market data, identify patterns, and execute trades automatically with speed and precision. This allows us to adapt quickly to market changes and achieve impressive results.

In addition, we also leverage IT for deep data analysis and machine learning. With this technology, we can uncover valuable insights from large datasets, including historical market data, economic indicators, and news sentiment analysis. This capability enables us to predict market trends more accurately and make more informed investment decisions. Our machine learning models continue to evolve, enhancing our trading strategy capabilities over time.

We welcome you to MLB Trade, where technology meets trading to create the extraordinary performance we have built over the past nine years.
